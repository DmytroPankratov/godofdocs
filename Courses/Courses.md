# Microsoft Learn Style and Voice Quick Start

Best Practices:
- **Check spelling and grammar**: Use tools like Microsoft Word to ensure accuracy.
- **Use a friendly, casual voice**: Write like you’re talking one-on-one with the reader.
- **Keep sentences simple**: Short, clear sentences make content easy to understand.
- **Focus on the customer’s intent**: Write with the specific task in mind.
- **Use everyday words**: Avoid jargon, but be technically accurate.
- **Write concisely**: Keep sentences and articles focused and to the point.
- **Make it easy to scan**: Organize steps logically and use visuals where needed.
- **Show empathy**: Be supportive and acknowledge challenges.

Localization Considerations:
- **Check for errors**: Ensure your content is free from spelling and grammar mistakes.
- **Keep sentences short**: Simplify compound sentences for easier translation.

For more details, refer to the [Microsoft Writing Style Guide](https://learn.microsoft.com/en-us/style-guide/)
